3:T53c,Celsius Network was a crypto lending platform that promised high yields on deposits. "Unbank yourself" was the slogan. CEO Alex Mashinsky became a YouTube personality, hosting weekly AMAs where he assured depositors their funds were safe. At its peak, Celsius held $20 billion in assets.

On June 12, 2022, Celsius froze all withdrawals. One month after LUNA collapsed and took Three Arrows Capital with it, Celsius could not meet redemptions. The platform that promised to replace banks had experienced a classic bank run.

The bankruptcy revealed that Celsius had been engaging in increasingly risky strategies to fund the yields it promised. It lent to hedge funds, invested in DeFi protocols, and used customer deposits in ways that would be illegal for any traditional bank. Mashinsky had personally withdrawn $10 million before the freeze.

Mashinsky was arrested in July 2023 and charged with fraud. In December 2024, he pleaded guilty and was sentenced to 12 years in prison. He had been selling CEL tokens while telling customers to buy, a textbook pump and dump.
