Advertising Policy

We maintain strict standards for advertisers to protect our readers and our reputation. All advertisements must comply with the following:

We Accept

Legitimate crypto exchanges and platforms (regulated or demonstrably credible)
DeFi protocols with audited smart contracts
Crypto infrastructure and tooling companies
Blockchain education and research platforms
Hardware wallets and security products
Web3 development tools and services
Crypto conferences and events

We Do NOT Accept

Unaudited tokens or active token sales without proper documentation
Obvious scam projects, ponzi schemes, or MLM crypto products
Gambling platforms (unless properly licensed)
Ads making unrealistic return promises ("100x guaranteed")
Anything that would embarrass us if it turned out to be a rug pull
Misleading, deceptive, or fraudulent advertisements
Ads for illegal products or services

CMZ reserves the right to reject or remove any advertisement at our sole discretion. We'd rather lose ad revenue than promote something that hurts our readers. Given that we literally run a Rug Pull Museum, the irony of promoting a rug pull would be... too much.

Editorial Independence

Advertising on CMZ does not include any editorial influence. Advertisers cannot:

Request, review, or approve editorial content

Request removal of articles or unfavorable coverage
Access editorial communications or plans
