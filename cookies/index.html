Cookie Policy

What cookies we use and why

Last updated: February 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and enable certain features to work properly.

CMZ uses cookies and similar technologies (pixels, web beacons, local storage) to provide, protect, and improve our services.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They include cookies for page navigation, security, and basic functionality.

Session cookies - Keep you connected as you browse pages
Security cookies - Protect against cross-site request forgery
Preference cookies - Remember sidebar state and dark mode settings
Analytics

Analytics & Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website. All data is aggregated and anonymous.

Google Analytics - Tracks page views, session duration, traffic sources, and user behavior patterns. Helps us improve content and user experience.
Advertising

Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used by advertising partners to deliver ads relevant to your interests. They may track your browsing activity across sites.

Google AdSense - Serves display advertisements. May use cookies to personalize ads based on your browsing history across websites.
DoubleClick - Google's ad serving technology. Tracks ad impressions and clicks.
Social

Social Media Cookies

These cookies are set by social media platforms when you use share buttons or interact with embedded content.

X (Twitter) - Share buttons and embedded tweets
Telegram - Share buttons for Telegram

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

View what cookies are stored on your device
Delete some or all cookies
Block all cookies or specific types
Set preferences for certain websites

Please note that blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. Essential cookies cannot be disabled without breaking core site features.

Browser-Specific Instructions

Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Google Ads Personalization

Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our practices. Check back periodically for updates.
