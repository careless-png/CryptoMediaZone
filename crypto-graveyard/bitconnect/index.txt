In January 2018, Texas and North Carolina regulators issued cease-and-desist orders. BitConnect shut down its lending platform overnight. BCC crashed from $363 to under $1 within hours. Billions in investor funds simply vanished.

The founder, Satish Kumbhani, was indicted on fraud charges by the DOJ. He disappeared. The FBI listed him as wanted. Promoters like Glenn Arcaro pled guilty and were sentenced to prison. Matos became a meme. The investors became cautionary tales.
