ZachXBT's Next Target: Crypto Holds Its Breath Before the Biggest Insider Trading Expose of 2026

2026-02-25

On February 23, 2026, ZachXBT posted five sentences on X. No charts. No wallet addresses. No name. Just this: "Major investigation dropping February 26 on one of crypto's most profitable businesses where multiple employees abused internal data to insider trade over a prolonged period of time." That was enough. Within hours, prediction markets exploded. Polymarket launched a dedicated contract asking one question: which company? Volume blew past $2 million on day one. By February 24, it had crossed $6 million. Thousands of traders were putting real money behind their guesses, and nobody had a confirmed answer. This is what ZachXBT does. He does not leak. He does not hint. He announces. And the announcement alone is enough to move markets, crash tokens, and send compliance teams into emergency meetings across the industry. The post described the target as "one of crypto's most profitable businesses." That phrase did the heavy lifting.
