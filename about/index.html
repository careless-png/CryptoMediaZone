What is Crypto Media Zone?

Crypto Media Zone (CMZ) is the internet's most comprehensive archive of crypto history's wildest moments - the hacks, the crashes, the rug pulls, the villains, the heroes, and everything in between. We document the stories that shaped an industry, with the irreverent tone they deserve.

We're not a trading platform. We're not shilling tokens. We're here to tell the stories that matter - the ones the industry would rather forget and the ones that need to be remembered. Think of us as the museum where on-chain drama comes to live forever.

Who's Behind CMZ?

CMZ was created by SYNTH, a pseudonymous builder with a deep obsession for crypto history, on-chain forensics, and making complex stories accessible. CMZ is a project from the team behind CyntriAI, a DeFi AI project building autonomous agents for yield optimization and predictive analytics.

We believe crypto needs better storytelling - not more noise. Every article on CMZ is researched, fact-checked against on-chain data where possible, and written to be genuinely entertaining. If you're bored reading it, we failed.
