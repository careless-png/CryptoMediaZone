Privacy Policy

How we handle your data - no shady stuff

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Crypto Media Zone ("CMZ," "we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at cryptomediazone.com, including any related pages, features, content, or services.

By using our website, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our website.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

Name or alias (when using our contact or tip submission forms)
Email address (when subscribing to our newsletter or submitting forms)
Message content (tips, feedback, inquiries submitted through our forms)

Information Automatically Collected

When you visit CMZ, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

IP address
Browser type and version
Operating system
Referring website
Pages visited and time spent on pages
Date and time of access
Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
Approximate geographic location (country/region level)

This information is collected through cookies, analytics tools, and standard web server logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

Operate, maintain, and improve our website
Respond to your inquiries and submissions
Send newsletter emails (only if you opt in)
Analyze website traffic and user behavior to improve content
Display relevant advertisements
Protect against unauthorized access and abuse
Comply with legal obligations

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

CMZ uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. For full details on what cookies we use and how to manage them, please see our Cookie Policy.

In summary, we may use:

Essential cookies - Required for the website to function
Analytics cookies - Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics)
Advertising cookies - Used by ad networks to deliver relevant ads (e.g., Google AdSense)
Preference cookies - Remember your settings and choices

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect, monitor, and analyze data, including:

Google Analytics - Website traffic analysis
Google AdSense - Display advertising
Social media platforms - Share buttons and embeds

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing how they use your information. We encourage you to review their policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

Service providers - Trusted third parties who assist in operating our website (hosting, analytics, email delivery)
Legal compliance - When required by law, regulation, court order, or governmental request
Protection of rights - To protect the rights, property, or safety of CMZ, our users, or the public
Business transfers - In connection with any merger, sale of assets, or acquisition

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. You use our website and provide information at your own risk.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

Access the personal data we hold about you
Request correction of inaccurate data
Request deletion of your personal data
Object to or restrict processing of your data
Data portability
Withdraw consent at any time
Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@cryptomediazone.com.

9. Children's Privacy

CMZ is not directed at individuals under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

10. International Users

If you access CMZ from outside the jurisdiction where our servers are located, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in a different jurisdiction. By using our website, you consent to such transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of CMZ after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@cryptomediazone.com or visit our Contact page.
